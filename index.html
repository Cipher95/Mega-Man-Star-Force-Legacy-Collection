ABOUT THE SERIES

What is Mega Man Star Force?

To celebrate Mega Man's 20th anniversary, the new titles Mega Man Star Force Pegasus, Leo, and Dragon were released simultaneously. They evolved the best features of the Battle Network series, adding a new 3D battle system and exciting gameplay mechanics.

The World of Star Force

It is the year 220X. The world is connected by a network of EM Waves. Our protagonist, Geo Stelar, still mourning the disappearance of his astronaut father, has his life changed forever when he encounters a rogue FM-ian named Omega-Xis.
